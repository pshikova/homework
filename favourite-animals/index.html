The relationship between the child and the cat

Cats and children usually get along well. But sometimes the child does not understand that he is torturing his beloved kitten by carrying it around the apartment, dressing it in doll clothes, playing with it for too long or inappropriately rough. Parents should explain to the child that the cat is not a toy, but a living creature with a delicate sensitivity that must be treated with respect. To get what you want from the cat, you need to win her sympathy, convince her that your desires coincide. Coercion, violence, threats and punishment (even more physical abuse) or changing your attitude towards her should never be allowed.

The cat in the home during the first weeks

The cat in the home during the first weeks It is rare to meet kittens to which the owners did not show caresses and attention in order to instill in them trust in humans. Basically, the cat is an almost perfect individualist. Entering the human home quite late, she has retained the charm of wild nature within herself. A truly caring owner should know that the cat is a creature with an independent life and should not interfere with it. Otherwise, severe mental and behavioral abnormalities are caused in these animals.
